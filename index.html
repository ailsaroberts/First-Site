Basic Sponge Recipe

This basic sponge cake is simple to master and an essential recipe for any baker. It can be flavoured, iced and served to suit any occasion.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 4oz/100g
Caster Sugar 4oz/100g
Eggs 2 medium
Self-raising flour 4oz/100g

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to gas mark 4/180C. Grease and line a 20cm cake tin or cupcake tray.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
  3. Beat in an egg, then sift in half of the flour and fold in. Repeat with the remaining eggs and flour.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared tin. Bake in the centre of the oven for 20-25 minutes for a sponge cake or 12-15 minutes for cupcakes. TEST: The cake is ready when a knife inserted into it comes out clean

Variations

Any flavour combinations can be added either to the sponge or filling. Examples include:

  • Chocolate sponge cake: Sift a tablespoon of cocoa powder into the sponge mix. Fill with chocolate buttercream or chocolate ganache.
  • Lemon drizzle cake: Add the zest of a lemon to the sponge mix. Make a syrup of lemon juice and caster sugar and drizzle over the cake whilst still warm
