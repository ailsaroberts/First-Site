Marshmallow Crispie Cakes

Ingredients

Oil
225g/9oz bag dairy toffees
4 tbsp butter
3 tbsp milk
1 cup (half a bag) marshmallows
6 cups rice crispies

Method

  1. Use a piece of kitchen roll soaked in oil to grease a heavy-based saucepan.
  2. Add the toffees to the pan with the milk and butter and melt over a low-medium heat
  3. When the toffees and butter have melted, add the marshmallows to the pan and stir over the heat until melted
  4. Remove from the heat and stir in the rice crispies
  5. Pour into a prepared 25x25cm tin and leave to set.
